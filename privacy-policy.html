Guess the Career

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Guess the Career (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use our mobile application (the “App”). We designed the App to work without creating a traditional account: there is no email or password sign-up.

1. Information we collect

Device identifier

When you first open the App, we generate a random device identifier and store it on your device. This identifier is sent to our backend to create and restore an anonymous profile across app sessions. We do not collect your phone number, email address, or real name unless you voluntarily choose a display name for leaderboards.

Gameplay and profile data

To provide game features, we store information such as:

  • Career Points, scores, streaks, and rounds completed
  • Game progress, including unlocked categories and difficulty settings
  • Active and completed game rounds, guesses, hints used, and chat messages during rounds
  • Daily challenge participation and completion
  • Leaderboard entries and optional display name
  • Career journal entries (discovered careers, fun facts, and related metadata)
  • In-app purchase and reward status (for example, whether ads have been removed)

Advertising data

The App displays ads through Google AdMob, including interstitial and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect or receive information such as device identifiers, IP address, ad interaction data, and approximate location to serve and measure ads. Google’s use of data is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

If you purchase “Remove Ads”, interstitial ads are disabled. Rewarded ads may remain optional depending on your choices in the App.

In-app purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. We receive purchase and entitlement status so we can apply benefits in the App (such as removing ads). We do not receive your full payment card details.

AI-generated content

Career clues and related game content may be generated using OpenAI services on our servers. When you play an AI-powered round, contextual information such as category, difficulty, language preference, and prior job titles to avoid repetition may be sent to OpenAI to generate clues. Job titles are not shown to you during an active round until the round ends.

OpenAI’s handling of API data is described in OpenAI’s Privacy Policy and applicable API terms.

Push notifications

If you enable notifications, we may send alerts about daily challenges. Your device’s push token may be used solely to deliver these notifications.

2. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the App and game features
  • Maintain your anonymous profile and progress across sessions
  • Calculate scores, streaks, leaderboards, and rewards
  • Verify in-app purchases and entitlements
  • Display advertising and optional rewarded ads
  • Generate AI career clues and related content
  • Protect against abuse, including rate limiting on AI endpoints
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Where data is stored

Game and profile data is stored on our self-hosted backend infrastructure (Google Cloud), in a database associated with your anonymous user ID. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

A device identifier is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. Authentication tokens used to access the backend are also stored on your device.

4. What we do not collect

  • We do not require an email address or password to use the App
  • We do not use Firebase Analytics in the App
  • We do not sell your personal information

5. Sharing with third parties

We share information only as needed to operate the App, including with:

  • Google Cloud — backend hosting and storage
  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase verification
  • OpenAI — server-side generation of career clues and related content
  • Apple / Google — app distribution and payment processing for purchases

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and our services.

6. Data retention

We retain gameplay and profile data for as long as needed to provide the App and maintain your progress. If you uninstall the App, data associated with your anonymous account may remain on our servers until deleted or anonymized.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your information.

Because accounts are anonymous and tied to a device identifier, we may need you to provide your in-app user ID or other details to locate your data. You can request deletion of your backend data by contacting us using the details below.

You can limit ad personalization through your device settings (for example, “Limit Ad Tracking” on iOS or advertising settings on Android).

8. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate. We take steps designed to protect your information consistent with this Privacy Policy.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including encrypted transport, server-side access controls, and rate limiting. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page and update the effective date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
